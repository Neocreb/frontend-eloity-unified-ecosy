## Getting Started

### Your Rewards Dashboard

When you first sign up, your Rewards Dashboard is automatically created. You'll see:

- **Recent Activities**: Your latest transactions

### Initial Benefits
- **Welcome Bonus**: 50 ELOITS to get started
- **New Account Bonus**: 1.5x multiplier for first 7 days

## Understanding Your Rewards

### ELOITS - Eloity's Native Currency

ELOITS is our platform's reward currency that represents your contributions to the ecosystem.

**Earning ELOITS**:
- Complete activities (posting, engaging, etc.)
- Refer friends
- Complete challenges
- Receive gifts from other users

**Using ELOITS**:
- Withdraw to real money
- Send as gifts to other users
- Unlock premium features
- Purchase digital products

### Types of Rewards

| Type | Description | Amount | Frequency |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------|
| Post Creation | Create a post | 10 ELOITS | Per post |
| Engagement | Like, comment, share | 2-5 ELOITS | Per action |
| Challenge Completion | Complete a challenge | 25-100 ELOITS | Per challenge |
| Referral | Friend signs up | 100 ELOITS | Per referral |
| First Purchase | Referred friend makes purchase | 50 ELOITS | Per purchase |
| Gifts Received | Another user sends you gift | Variable | Per gift |

- **Social Butterfly**: Get 10 friends

### 5. Marketplace
- **Sell Products**: 5% commission on sales price

- **Provide Services**: Variable based on service

### 6. Gifts
- **Receive Gifts**: Instant balance credit
- **Send Gifts**: Deduct from your balance
- **Gift Reactions**: +5 ELOITS for appreciating gifts

## Trust Score System

Your Trust Score affects how many ELOITS you can earn and unlock features.

### Understanding Trust Score

**Score Range**: 0-100
**Starting Score**: 50
**What It Represents**: Your reliability and activity quality

### Factors Affecting Trust Score

| Factor | Weight | How to Improve |
|--------|--------|---|
| Engagement Quality | 40% | Post engaging content, get likes |
| Activity Consistency | 20% | Log in regularly, maintain activity |
| Peer Validation | 20% | Get follows, comments from others |
| Profile Completeness | 10% | Complete profile info |
| Spam Incidents | -10% | Avoid spam, violations |

### Trust Tiers & Benefits

| Tier | Score | Daily Cap | Benefits |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|
| Beginner | 0-20 | 100 ELOITS | Basic features |
| Novice | 21-40 | 250 ELOITS | Challenges unlocked |
| Trusted | 41-70 | 500 ELOITS | Referrals, marketplace |
| Expert | 71-85 | 750 ELOITS | Premium challenges |
| Master | 86-100 | 1000 ELOITS | All features, best rates |

### Improving Your Trust Score

1. **Post Quality Content**: Share valuable, engaging content
2. **Engage With Others**: Like, comment, and share others' posts
3. **Maintain Consistency**: Log in regularly, stay active
4. **Follow Community Rules**: Avoid spam, harassment, or violations
5. **Complete Profile**: Add profile photo, bio, links
6. **Time Investment**: New accounts get +10 bonus points

### Activity Decay

Inactive accounts lose trust points:
- 1 point per 10 days of inactivity
- Login streaks reset after 7 days
- Return to activity to recover points

## Referral Program

### Getting Started

1. **Find Your Code**: Go to Rewards > Referrals
2. **Share Your Link**: Copy and share with friends
3. **Earn**: Get paid when friends sign up and spend

### Your Referral Link

```
https://eloity.com/join?ref=YOUR_CODE
```

### Earning Structure

**Tier-Based Commission**:
- **Bronze** (0 ELOITS): 5% commission
- **Silver** ($5K+ earned): 7.5% commission
- **Gold** ($25K+ earned): 10% commission
- **Platinum** ($100K+ earned): 15% commission

### How Earnings Work

1. **Friend signs up**: You earn 100 ELOITS instantly
2. **Friend makes first purchase**: You earn 50 ELOITS + commission
3. **Ongoing**: You earn % of their activities

### Auto-Share Feature

Auto-share distributes your referral earnings automatically:
- Set percentage (0-100%)
- Earnings split between you and referral
- Track auto-share total in statistics

### Referral Statistics

Track your performance:
- Total referrals
- Active referrals
- Total earnings this month
- Current tier & next tier requirements
- Top referrers leaderboard

### Growing Your Network

1. **Quality over Quantity**: Engaged friends = better earnings
2. **Share Your Success**: Show friends your earnings
3. **Community Invites**: Use referral events
4. **Social Sharing**: Share on other platforms

## Withdrawals

### Methods Supported

1. **Bank Transfer** (2-3 days)
2. **PayPal** (Instant)
3. **Crypto Wallet** (Varies)
4. **Gift Card** (Email instant)

### Minimum & Limits

- **Minimum**: 100 ELOITS (~$5)
- **Maximum per Request**: Your balance
- **Per Day Limit**: Based on tier
- **Processing Time**: 1-3 business days

### Withdrawal Fees

| Method | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| Bank Transfer | 2% |
| PayPal | 2.5% |
| Crypto | 1% |
| Gift Card | 0% |

### How to Withdraw

1. **Go to Rewards** > "Withdraw"
2. **Choose Method**: Select payment method
3. **Enter Amount**: Amount in ELOITS
4. **Review Fee**: See final amount received
5. **Confirm**: Verify and submit
6. **Approval**: Admin reviews in 24 hours
7. **Payment**: Sent to your account

### Example

| Amount | Method | Fee | You Receive |
|--------|--------|-----|-------------|
| 1000 | Bank | 2% | 980 |
| 1000 | PayPal | 2.5% | 975 |
| 1000 | Crypto | 1% | 990 |
| 1000 | Card | 0% | 1000 |

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How do I get ELOITS?**
A: Complete activities like posting, engaging, referring friends, or completing challenges.

**Q: Can I lose ELOITS?**
A: Only when you withdraw or send gifts. Daily caps limit earnings based on trust score.

**Q: What's the difference between ELOITS and money?**
A: ELOITS are our platform currency. You convert them to real money through withdrawal.

**Q: How often are earnings added?**
A: Instantly when activity is completed and verified.

### Trust Score

**Q: Why is my trust score low?**
A: Could be new account, low engagement, or inactivity. Post quality content and stay active.

**Q: Can I recover a low score?**
A: Yes! Regular activity, quality engagement, and following rules will improve your score.

**Q: How do I increase my daily cap?**
A: Improve your trust score by engaging quality content and staying active.

### Referrals

**Q: How much can I earn from referrals?**
A: Depends on your tier and their activity. Commission ranges from 5-15%.

**Q: When do I get the referral bonus?**
A: 100 ELOITS when they sign up, 50 when they make first purchase.

**Q: Can I track my referrals?**
A: Yes, detailed statistics in Rewards > Referrals section.

### Withdrawals

**Q: What's the minimum withdrawal?**
A: 100 ELOITS (approximately $5).

**Q: How long does withdrawal take?**
A: 1-3 business days depending on payment method.

**Q: Are there withdrawal fees?**
A: Yes, 2% for bank transfers, varies by method.

**Q: What if my withdrawal is rejected?**
A: Usually due to invalid account info. Update your payment method and try again.

### Challenges & Levels

**Q: Can I do challenges unlimited times?**
A: Most can only be completed once. Special seasonal challenges reset periodically.

**Q: How do I level up?**
A: Earn ELOITS. Each level has an earnings threshold.

**Q: What do levels give me?**
A: Higher daily caps, exclusive challenges, and better commission rates.

### Account Safety

**Q: How are my earnings protected?**
A: Your balance is secured in Supabase with encryption and RLS policies.

**Q: Can my account be hacked?**
A: We use industry-standard security. Enable 2FA for extra protection.

**Q: What if my balance is wrong?**
A: Contact support with evidence. We maintain audit logs of all transactions.
